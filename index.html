FAQ

Common Questions

What file formats do you accept?

We accept STL, OBJ, STEP, and most common 3D formats. If you have something else, send it on WhatsApp and we'll confirm right away.

How do I send you my file?

Send your file directly on WhatsApp. We'll review it, confirm printability, and reply with a quote — usually within minutes.

How long does printing take?

Most orders are ready within 24–48 hours depending on size and complexity. We'll give you an accurate time estimate with your quote.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on material, size, and complexity. Send us your file or describe what you need and we'll send a quote with no obligation.

Can you design something from scratch?

Yes. Share a sketch, reference photo, or describe your idea on WhatsApp. We'll model it and send you a preview before we print anything.

My file is broken or won't slice — can you fix it?

Absolutely. Send the file over WhatsApp and we'll diagnose and repair mesh errors, non-manifold geometry, and other common issues at no extra charge.
